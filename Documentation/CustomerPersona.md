# Customer Persona: Mercy Melon

## Basic Profile
- Name: Mercy Melon  
- Age: 26–35  
- Role: Social Media Manager / Content Strategist  
- Industry: Marketing / Advertising  
- Location: Urban (e.g., Bangalore, Mumbai)  
- Experience Level: Mid-level (3–8 years)  

---

## Professional Background
Mercy works in a fast-paced marketing team where she is responsible for:
- Creating social media content (captions, posts, campaigns)  
- Managing brand voice and tone  
- Coordinating with designers and marketing managers  
- Meeting tight deadlines and content calendars  

She regularly uses AI tools like ChatGPT for:
- Generating first drafts  
- Brainstorming ideas  
- Creating variations quickly  

However, she manually reviews and refines content before publishing.

---

## Goals
- Produce engaging and high-quality content quickly  
- Maintain brand consistency  
- Avoid public backlash or negative feedback  
- Ensure cultural sensitivity and inclusivity  
- Improve efficiency without compromising quality  

---

## Pain Points
- Limited time for deep content review  
- Risk of unintentional bias in AI-generated content  
- Difficulty ensuring cultural relevance  
- Pressure from instant social media feedback  
- Multiple approval layers  

---

## Behavior & Attitude
- Uses AI as a support tool, not a replacement  
- Relies on human judgment for final decisions  
- Aware of bias risks in marketing content  
- Values feedback and collaboration  

---

## Needs
- A tool that flags biased or sensitive content  
- Fast analysis within tight deadlines  
- Understanding of local and cultural context  
- Helps maintain consistency across campaigns  
- Reduces risk of brand reputation damage  

---

## Challenges with AI Tools
- May be too Western-centric  
- May miss cultural nuances  
- Difficulty handling subjective context  

---

## Conclusion
Mercy prefers using AI as a first-level filtering tool, while relying on human judgment for final approval.
