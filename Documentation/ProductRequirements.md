
# Product Requirements

## Overview
AdEthix AI is an AI-based system that analyzes marketing content and detects bias, stereotypes, and unethical messaging before it is published.

---

## Objectives
- Identify biased or insensitive language  
- Ensure ethical and inclusive communication  
- Assist marketing teams in content review  
- Reduce risk of negative audience reactions  

---

## Features

### 1. Content Input
- Users can enter marketing text such as ads, captions, or product descriptions  

### 2. AI Analysis
- The system analyzes the content for:
  - Bias  
  - Stereotypes  
  - Cultural insensitivity  
  - Misleading claims  

### 3. Risk Detection
- Provides output as:
  - Low Risk  
  - Medium Risk  
  - High Risk  

### 4. Suggestions
- Suggests improved or safer wording  
- Highlights problematic phrases  

---

## Input
- Text input (marketing content)

## Output
- Risk level  
- Highlighted issues  
- Suggested corrections  

---

## Users
- Marketing teams  
- Advertising agencies  
- Content creators  
- Social media managers  

---

## Future Enhancements
- Integration with social media platforms  
- Real-time content checking  
- Multilingual support
